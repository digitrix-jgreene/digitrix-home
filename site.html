Who We Are

Digitrix LLC is a minority- and woman-owned company. We specialize in digital transformation, technology implementation, and strategic consulting for small businesses and public sector organizations.

We are problem solvers who are motivated by mission. We love building sustainable, cutting-edge digital solutions. Our team brings deep expertise in modern web design, cloud architecture, software development, and process optimization. We are ready to help your organization navigate the complexities of the digital age.

Here's what we're not.

We're not your typical technology company who will try to do everything and will do anything. We're intentional with who we hire, who we partner with, and we believe deeply in the solutions we create. We seek problems that we can obsess over and will challenge us, all while bringing growth to our people. Our unique approach allows our customers to be confident that they are always getting the A-Team while keeping our people engaged, fulfilled, evolving, and connected.

Our greatest asset is our people. These are the values we live by.

Show Up Authentically
We embrace an authentic culture. Authenticity allows us to connect as humans and create a fulfilling work environment that boosts growth, success, and overall happiness. We value each individual for their unique skillsets and the many other dimensions they bring to the table.
Lead With Curiosity
We rely on the power of learning, exploring novel possibilities, and staying curious to spur creativity and innovation. We invest deeply in the evolution of our team, recognizing that the continuous mastery of new technologies is the primary engine behind the sustainable, cutting-edge solutions we build for our clients.
Collaboration Is Key
Our impact depends on our ability to collaborate with people from diverse disciplines and backgrounds. We seek different perspectives, ideas, and expertise to drive innovation and deliver the best results. Ultimately, we believe the best solutions are co-authored, born from a culture where every voice is heard and every perspective serves a purpose.
